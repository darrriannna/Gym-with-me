My weekly workout routine!

Monday: Legs and Glutes

  1. Warm Up Wide Stance Bodyweight Squat: 2 sets x 12-15 reps
  2. Wide Stance Barbell Squat: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  3. Romanian Deadlift: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  4. Barbell Glute Bridge: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  5. Glute Kickback: 4 sets x 12-15 reps (each leg)

Tuesday: Recovery

Wednesday: Core and LISS Cardio

  1. Warm Up Crunches: 2 sets x 10 reps
  2. Roman Chair Leg Raise: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  3. LISS Cardio - Treadmill/Bike - Moderate: 30 minutes

Thursday: Legs

  1. Warm Up Stiff-Legged Barbell Deadlift: 2 sets x 12-20 reps
  2. Stiff-Legged Barbell Deadlift: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  3. Barbell Glute Bridge: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  4. Reverse Lunge: 4 sets x 8-12 reps (each leg)

Friday: Upper Body + HIIT Cardio

  1. Warm Up standing Barbell Shoulder Press: 2 sets x 12-15 reps
  2. Standing Barbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  3. Side Lateral Raise: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  4. Tricep Dips: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  5. HIIT Cardio - Treadmill/Bike - Vigorous: 20 seconds of work + 40 seconds of recovery
